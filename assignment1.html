kiran bedi

Kiran Bedi was born on the 9th of June 1949 in Punjab. She took an active part in National Cadet Corps in her schooling days. Kiran Bedi completed her Bachelors in English in 1968. She did her graduation in Government College for Women in Amritsar. Kiran Bedi has faced many challenges in her life. Also, she undertook tasks with great willpower and determination.

GREAT ACHIEVEMENTS

  1. SHE WAS THE NATIONAL WOMEN’S LAWN TENNIS CHAMPION
  2. KIRAN BEDI BECAME THE FIRST FEMALE IPS OFFICER IN INDIA
  • SHE WAS AWARDED THE PRESIDENT’S POLICE MEDAL FOR GALLANTRY IN 1979
  • SHE LAUNCHED A CAMPAIGN AGAINST DRUG ABUSE WHICH EVOLVED INTO AN ORGANIZATION
